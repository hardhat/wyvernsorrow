# Comedic Characters and Enemies

Based on the high-fantasy setting with a comedic twist.

## NPCs (The Townspeople)

### The Merchant (WNPC_MERCHANT)
- **High Fantasy Aspect**: A legendary trader of mystical artifacts who has seen a thousand kingdoms fall.
- **Comedic Twist**: He is absolutely obsessed with "loyalty points" and refuses to sell a world-saving sword unless you have a "Gold-Tier Adventure Card." He treats the demon invasion as a "minor supply chain disruption."
- **Voice**: Overly formal corporate speak mixed with archaic wizard-talk.

### The Father / Blacksmith (WNPC_FATHER)
- **High Fantasy Aspect**: A master smith who can forge blades from starlight.
- **Comedic Twist**: He’s an extreme helicopter parent. He only sharpens your sword if you promise to "wear a scarf" and "be home by eight." He once tried to forge a "safety-shield" that was just a giant pillow.
- **Voice**: Gruff but constantly worrying/fretting.

### The Mother / Healer (WNPC_MOTHER)
- **High Fantasy Aspect**: A high priestess of the eternal flame with unmatched restorative powers.
- **Comedic Twist**: She heals people by aggressively "guilting" the wounds away. "Oh, you got stabbed by an Orc? Well, I had to stay up all night wondering where you were, so who's the real victim here?"
- **Voice**: Sweet, melodic, but deeply passive-aggressive.

### The Old Man / Alchemist (WNPC_OLD_MAN)
- **High Fantasy Aspect**: A sage with knowledge of every potion and poison in existence.
- **Comedic Twist**: He is catastrophically forgetful and often confuses "Potion of Strength" with "Potion of Looking Exactly Like a Large Cabbage." He measures ingredients in "vibes" rather than ounces.
- **Voice**: Shaky, wandering off-topic mid-sentence.

### The Little Girl (WNPC_LITTLE_GIRL)
- **High Fantasy Aspect**: An innocent soul destined to bring balance to the realm.
- **Comedic Twist**: She is actually the most cold-blooded strategist in the party. She gives "adorable" drawings to enemies that are actually detailed tactical maps of their weaknesses.
- **Voice**: High-pitched and cute, but says terrifyingly calculated things.

## Enemies and Bosses

### Werewolf (WENT_WEREWOLF)
- **Comedic Twist**: A very "good boy" who is deeply conflicted. He wants to tear your throat out, but he *really* wants someone to throw a stick. He keeps accidentally wagging his tail during his "fearsome" transformation.

### Ogre Boss (WBOSS_OGRE)
- **Comedic Twist**: An aspiring gourmet chef. He’s not trying to eat you because he’s evil; he’s trying to find the "perfect seasoning" for adventurer meat. He gets very offended if you don't compliment his "reduction of swamp-moss."

### Skeleton (WENT_SKELETON)
- **Comedic Twist**: Constantly suffering from "bone-identity" issues. Skeletons often accidentally swap limbs mid-battle and spend three turns arguing about whose left tibia is whose.

### Genie (WENT_GENIE)
- **Comedic Twist**: A disgruntled bureaucrat of the lamp. He hates his job and tries to fulfill wishes in the most literal, annoying way possible to encourage people to stop rubbing things.

### The Demon Lord (WDEMONLORD)
- **High Fantasy Aspect**: The ultimate harbinger of doom, draped in shadows and malice.
- **Comedic Twist**: He’s an extreme micromanager who is currently going through a mid-life crisis. He’s tired of the "darkness" and really wants to open a small artisanal pottery shop in the Town. He only fights you because his "Dark Generals" would make fun of him if he didn't.
- **Voice**: Deep and booming, but frequently complains about his lower back pain and the "entitled youth" of today's heroes.

### Land Dragon (WBOSS_LAND_DRAGON)
- **High Fantasy Aspect**: A primordial beast fused with the bedrock of the world, guarding the ancient pass.
- **Comedic Twist**: He thinks he's invisible if he stays perfectly still, even though he's the size of a house. He gets extremely embarrassed if you point out he's not a rock. "If I cannot see you, you cannot see me. I am a boulder. Move along."
- **Voice**: Slow, rumbling, with very long pauses.

### Fire Dragon (WBOSS_FIRE_DRAGON)
- **High Fantasy Aspect**: A living furnace that dwells in the heart of the volcano, capable of melting mountains.
- **Comedic Twist**: He suffers from chronic, soul-searing heartburn and is constantly searching for "Gaviscon-grade" calcium deposits. He’s actually a sensitive artist who uses his breath for "extreme glass-blowing."
- **Voice**: Aggressive and "hissing," but prone to suddenly talking about his "delicate craft."

### Wind Dragon (WBOSS_WIND_DRAGON)
- **High Fantasy Aspect**: A majestic creature of the clouds, master of the hurricanes and the sky.
- **Comedic Twist**: He is the realm's biggest gossip because he hears everything "on the wind." He will literally stop a battle mid-swing if you tell him something juicy about the Demon Lord’s secret diary.
- **Voice**: Breezy and light, constantly whispering "did you hear about...?"

### Pumpkin Head (WBOSS_PUMPKIN_HEAD)
- **High Fantasy Aspect**: A cursed entity of the harvest, wielding dark magic in the misty swamp.
- **Comedic Twist**: He is deeply offended by "Pumpkin Spice" season. He spent centuries cultivating a "fearsome" reputation, only to be turned into a trendy latte flavor. He’s currently filing a realm-wide lawsuit for "unauthorized use of my likeness."
- **Voice**: Hollow and echoing, but sounds like a very stressed, overworked lawyer.
